Commonly Asked Questions

How does ResumeAnalyzer work?
Our AI analyzer compares your resume against real-world job descriptions, looking for key skills to give you a matching score.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We use Supabase which provides enterprise-security features and excrypted data storage. Your resumes are only stored if you choose to save them on your account.
What file formats are supported?
We currently support PDF and Word Docx files. PDF is recommended for layout consistency.
How often can I analyze my resume?
There are no limits! Tweak and re-run your resume as many times as you need to improve your resume matching score!
Why is my matching percentage low?
This happens if specific keywords from the job description are missing. Use our AI Career Coach to find missing gaps!
Can I save job listings?
Yes! Use the "Saved Jobs" tab to track positions you like.
Can I prepare for interviews here?
Yes, the AI Career Coach can provide mock interview questions based on the job you're targeting.
What if the AI Career Coach stop responding?
Try refreshing the page. If issues persist, please contact us.
Can I manage multiple resumes?
Currently, we analyze one resume at a time. Tailoring your master resume for each job gives the best results.
Is there a mobile app?
We are web-based and mobile-optimized! A standalone app is in development.
How do I delete my account?
Please email us and we will process your deletion request within 48 hours.
How do I contact the AI Career Coach?
Click the "AI Coach" tab in your sidebar to start a real-time session.
